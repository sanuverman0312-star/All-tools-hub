Introduction

At All Tools Hub by Saanu, we respect your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our free online tools. Our core principle is minimal data collection and maximum security.

We do not collect, store, or sell your personal input data. Your files and text are processed directly in your browser.

Data Collection & Usage

We operate a client-side first platform. This means:

  • Input Data: The text, images, or files you process are manipulated using JavaScript within your own device. They are NOT uploaded to our servers for processing.
  • Personal Info: We do not require registration, so we do not collect names, emails, or phone numbers.
  • Logs: Like most websites, our hosting provider may collect standard server logs (IP address, browser type) for security and maintenance purposes.

Cookies & Local Storage

We use local storage technology (similar to cookies) strictly for functional purposes:

  • Preferences: To remember your theme preference (Dark/Light mode).
  • Tool State: Some tools may temporarily save your input locally so you don't lose work if you refresh, but this stays on your device.

Third-Party Services

To provide a better experience, we use trusted third-party services:

  • Font Awesome / Google Fonts: For icons and typography (loaded via CDN).
  • QR Code API: For the QR Code Generator, we may use a public API to generate the image.
  • Analytics: We may use basic, anonymized analytics to see which tools are most popular.
