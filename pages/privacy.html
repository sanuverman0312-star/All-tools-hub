Introduction

Welcome to All Tools Hub by Saanu ("we," "our," or "I"). I am committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our website and tools.

This Privacy Policy explains how I handle information when you visit All Tools Hub (alltoolshub.com). I believe in transparency and want you to understand exactly what happens when you use our services.

üéØ Our Privacy Promise

I don't collect, store, or sell your personal data. All tool processing happens entirely in your browser, meaning your data never leaves your device.

Information We Collect

The short answer: Almost nothing. I've designed All Tools Hub to minimize data collection as much as possible. Here's what you should know:

Information We DON'T Collect:

  • Personal Information: No names, email addresses, phone numbers, or addresses
  • Tool Input Data: Text, images, or files you process with our tools
  • Account Information: No accounts, no logins, no registrations
  • Payment Information: All tools are free, no payments needed
  • Location Data: We don't track your precise location

Information That May Be Collected:

  • Anonymous Analytics: General usage statistics (page views, tool popularity) without personal identification
  • Technical Information: Browser type, device type (for compatibility purposes only)
  • Preference Data: Theme settings stored locally in your browser

How Our Tools Work

This is the most important part of our privacy policy. All tools on All Tools Hub are designed to process data entirely within your browser. This means:

  • Text Tools: Your text is processed locally. Word counts, conversions, and formatting happen in your browser.
  • Image Tools: Images are processed using JavaScript in your browser. They are never uploaded to any server.
  • Converters: All conversion calculations happen client-side. Your data stays on your device.
  • Generators: Passwords, UUIDs, and other generated content are created in your browser using local algorithms.

üîê What This Means For You

When you use our tools, your sensitive data (passwords, personal text, images, etc.) never touches our servers. It stays completely private on your device. You could even use most of our tools offline!

Cookies & Local Storage

I use minimal cookies and local storage to enhance your experience:

  • Theme Preference: Remembers if you prefer dark or light mode
  • Tool Settings: Some tools may save your preferred settings locally

These are stored locally in your browser's localStorage and are not sent to any server. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

We do NOT use:

  • Tracking cookies
  • Advertising cookies
  • Third-party marketing cookies
  • Cross-site tracking

Third-Party Services

I may use limited third-party services to provide certain functionality:

  • QR Code Generation: For QR codes, we use external APIs (like qrserver.com) which only receives the text/URL you want to encode - no personal information.
  • Font Icons: We use Font Awesome and Google Fonts for icons and typography. These are loaded from CDNs and don't track you.
  • Analytics (Optional): We may use privacy-focused analytics (like Google Analytics with IP anonymization) to understand general usage patterns.

I carefully select third-party services that respect user privacy and do not engage in invasive tracking.

Data Security

Security is built into the foundation of All Tools Hub:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All connections to our website are encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • No Server Storage: Since we don't collect data, there's no database to breach
  • Client-Side Processing: Your data stays in your browser, under your control
  • Regular Updates: I keep all dependencies and security measures up to date

üí° Best Practices

While we prioritize security, I recommend not using any online tool for extremely sensitive information like passwords to critical accounts. Our Password Generator creates passwords locally, but always use a trusted password manager for storage.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Know what data (if any) I have about you (spoiler: it's none)
  • Deletion: Clear your local preferences by clearing browser data
  • Opt-Out: Disable cookies in your browser settings
  • Ask Questions: Contact me anytime with privacy concerns

Since I don't collect personal data, most traditional privacy rights (like data portability) don't apply here - but that's a good thing!

Children's Privacy

All Tools Hub is designed to be safe for users of all ages. I do not knowingly collect any information from children or adults. The tools are educational and safe for everyone to use.

Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When I do:

  • The "Last Updated" date at the top will change
  • For significant changes, I may add a notice on the homepage
  • The previous versions will be archived

I encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how I protect your privacy.
